Avant Lubalin

A type study of the two fonts Avant Garde and Lubalin Graph. Both are expressive geometric fonts created in the 70s' by Herb Lubalin & Tom Carnase. This book was influenced by Vice President Spiro Agnew; a speech about the youth of the time.
