Cutty Sark

The object of the project was to recreate a fresh identity for the existing company, Cutty Sark. Intentions of this project were to establish the brand in a unique cohesive manner. All the while holding existing and creating new target markets.

Project assets include and original logo, with corresponding tagline and stationery. A variety of packaging for multiple products, environmental advertising, and a cross platform website.
