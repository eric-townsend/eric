Tenderloin

For this assignment the mission was to create an engaging campaign that benefits San Francisco with the use of modern day technology.
I choose to tackle the Tenderloin, a lower income district area that is glowing with creativity and hidden gems.

This application allows users to explore multiple routes within different categories, upload your own routes, and manipulate existing routes along the way.
The Tenderloin is a problem that San Francisco tends to sweep under the rug, by encouraging people to explore and find hidden gems we are one step closer to becoming a smarter San Francisco.
